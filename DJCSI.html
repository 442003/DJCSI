About the Commitee

Formed in 1965, the CSI has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path since its formative years. Today, the CSI has 71 chapters all over India, 418 student branches, and more than 90000 members including India's most famous IT industry leaders, brilliant scientists and dedicated academicians.At D. J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, the students of department have formed their own student’s chapter ‘DJSCE-CSI’ for overall development of students. The mission of the DJSCE-CSI chapter is to facilitate research, knowledge sharing, learning and career enhancement for all categories of IT professionals, while simultaneously inspiring and nurturing students and helping them to integrate into the IT community.
